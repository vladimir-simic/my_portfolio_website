Who am I?

I am a graphic designer and a self-taught front-end developer. I recently relocated to Riga with my family. For the past seven years I have worked in a design and pre-press studio in Belgrade (Serbia), where I was responsible for variety of design and technical jobs, such as graphic design and pre-press production with Adobe Illustrator and CorelDraw. Considering that I am a fast learner, energetic, persistant at work, and curious person, I was interested and motivated to learn by myself other software and new working techniques such as HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Beside graphic design experience, I have certain experience in web design. I still need and want to learn and I am ready to have a cooperation with individual clints as well as with start up and newborn companies, and also with developed companies. Through my web presentation, you can see some interesting works of mine.
